What is the significance of the secondary camera in the rear dual-camera set up?

The secondary camera senses the depth of the image for the bokeh effect. This helps achieve a professional level finesse in your photos.

How many camera modes are available in Honor 7x?

Honor 7x offers more than 15 modes for the rear camera like portrait mode, light painting, documents scan, good food etc. It has more than 6 modes for the front camera including panorama, time lapse, audio note etc.

Apart from unlocking the phone, what all we can do with finger print sensor?

You can capture photos, browse gallery, answer calls, stop alarms and even operate the notification panel.

How Honor 7x gives you a better performance?

Honor 7x is powered by Kirin 659 Octa Core 2.36GHz processor along with i5 co-processor which gives you a lag-free experience as i5 co-processor remains active all the time supplementing the CPU tasks like navigation, step counting etc.

What makes Honor 7x perfect for gaming and watching videos?

The phone comes with 18:9 full view display along with screen-to-body ratio of 82.9% which gives you a wider screen for gaming and video playing while still keeping it handy.
